Figma vs Sketch

Cloud-native vs Mac-only design tool.

Figma has largely won this war with real-time collaboration and browser access. Sketch is mac-only, feature-rich, but lost ground.

At a glance

FigmaSketch
PlatformAll platformsMac only
CollaborationReal-time, best in classNeeds plugins
PricingFree tier + paid$$

When to pick Figma

Teams, cross-platform, real-time collab.

When to pick Sketch

Mac-only team, established Sketch workflows.

My verdict

Figma for nearly all new projects.
