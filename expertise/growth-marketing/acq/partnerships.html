Partnerships

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Partnerships reach audiences you don't have access to. Done right, they're high-leverage. Done wrong, they're busywork.

Types

  • Integration partnerships (Zapier, ecosystems)
  • Content co-marketing (guest posts, webinars)
  • Affiliate programs
  • White-label
  • Reseller / channel

Evaluation

  • Does partner audience overlap with your ICP?
  • Is there mutual value (not just you getting leads)?
  • What's the realistic volume?

Structure

  • Joint content / webinars
  • Newsletter swaps
  • Referral revenue share
  • Product integrations

Common mistakes

  • Vague partnerships without clear deliverables
  • Too many small partnerships spread thin
  • Not tracking source of users
