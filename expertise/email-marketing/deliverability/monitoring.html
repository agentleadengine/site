Monitoring placement

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Your email tool reports 'delivered', but delivered could mean inbox or spam folder. Monitor actual placement.

Tools

  • Google Postmaster Tools
  • Glock Apps inbox placement tests
  • Seed addresses (inbox accounts you check manually)

Signals to watch

  • Inbox placement rate
  • Gmail spam folder rate
  • Junk folder at Outlook

Actions

  • Placement below 90%? Fix before scaling
  • Sudden drop? Investigate recent sends
  • Quarterly audit

What to do with this

  • Schedule the 15-minute weekly check as a calendar block, skipping it catches problems 2-4 weeks late
  • Set automated alerts in your ESP for thresholds (bounce, complaint, placement), alerts catch issues before your weekly review
  • Test inbox placement from multiple seed lists, single-source monitoring misses provider-specific deliverability issues
  • When metrics drift, pause that domain same-day, continuing to send through degradation compounds the reputation damage
  • Quarterly deep audit: review all domains, segment send rates, and full authentication config, catches drift annual reviews miss
