Close vs HubSpot

Inside-sales-focused vs full-stack CRM.

Close is built for calling/SDR-heavy teams with powerful call and email features. HubSpot is broader.

At a glance

CloseHubSpot
CallingNative + strongAdd-on
Cold emailBuilt-inAdd-on
Marketing hubNoneFull
PriceFrom $49/userFrom $0, scales

When to pick Close

Inside sales team, heavy calling + cold email, want one tool.

When to pick HubSpot

Marketing + sales integration, service functions.

My verdict

Close if your team lives on phones and cold email. HubSpot if marketing alignment matters.
