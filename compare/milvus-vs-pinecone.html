Milvus vs Pinecone

Enterprise open-source vs managed leader.

Milvus is built for enormous scale (billion+ vectors) but requires significant operational investment. Pinecone gives you scale without the ops.

At a glance

MilvusPinecone
Scale ceilingBillions+Billions+
Ops burdenHighNone
Cost at scaleCompute onlyHigher
Learning curveSteepGentle

When to pick Milvus

Extreme scale where ops cost is less than managed cost.

When to pick Pinecone

Managed simplicity, easier hiring.

My verdict

Milvus when you have dedicated infra teams. Pinecone when you don't.
