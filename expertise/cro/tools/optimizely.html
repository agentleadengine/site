Optimizely

📖 3 min readUpdated 2026-04-19

Optimizely is the enterprise CRO platform. Deep features, large companies, premium pricing.

Strengths

  • Feature breadth
  • Performance at scale
  • Feature flags + experimentation in one

Weaknesses

  • Expensive
  • Overkill for most sites
  • Requires significant setup

What to do with this

  • Use Optimizely if you have enterprise scale + engineering capacity, its full-stack server-side testing is the differentiator
  • Skip Optimizely at sub-enterprise scale, simpler tools produce equivalent results at a fraction of cost
  • Budget for the implementation time, Optimizely's setup is non-trivial and requires engineering integration
  • Pair with a dedicated experimentation lead, the tool's depth requires specialist attention
  • Consider cheaper alternatives (VWO, Convert) first if budget matters, Optimizely's premium price targets a specific buyer profile
